## Strategic Implications

**1. Mojtaba Khamenei's "should not be sent abroad at all" HEU directive against Trump's "DESTROYED" Truth Social demand: principals publicly stake incompatible positions on the MOU's load-bearing nuclear clause inside the same 48-hour window.**

The 48 hours between Day 92's MOU terms-naming and Day 93's principal-position contestation is the deal cycle's sharpest reversal on a load-bearing clause. Per Euronews citing Iranian officials and Tasnim's continued framing, Supreme Leader Mojtaba Khamenei has directed that Iran's enriched uranium "should not be sent abroad at all" — a position incompatible with the MOU's named top-priority 60-day enriched-uranium removal clause (Euronews, CNBC). Pezeshkian publicly stated no major decision would be made without the Supreme Leader's approval, closing the political-cover space the Doha delegation had used to keep the file moving. Trump's Truth Social demand that HEU be "DESTROYED" is a stronger formulation than "removal" and is itself incompatible with the Mojtaba directive — destruction-in-place and non-export-with-non-destruction are opposed mechanisms with no obvious middle. IAEA Grossi's standing assessment that "much of Iran's enriched uranium likely still resides at Isfahan" frames the physical-disposition problem the MOU clause now contests politically (Foreign Policy carry). Analytical judgment: under the multi-clock framework the negotiation-capacity clock worsened sharply. Skeptical counter: Iranian officials have raised disposition objections to HEU clauses in prior negotiating rounds (Geneva, Doha early May) that subsequently narrowed; Trump's "DESTROYED" may be public bargaining posture rather than the negotiated US position. But Round 4 is four calendar days out — the public-position floor is now set inside that interval.

Skeptical counter: re-escalation rhetoric has been deployed as MOU-clearance pressure since Day 86 without operationalization, and Hegseth's "any deal will be a good deal" framing publicly leaves room for signoff.
