## Current war status

Day 90 flips escalation direction from mixed to escalating. Mousavi called negotiations "pure loss," Fars relayed a Guard claim of having shot down an MQ-9 and fired at a second MQ-9 and an F-35, and Mojtaba Khamenei extended his Day 89 line to vow the US "will no longer have a safe haven" in the region (Euronews, Iranwire, NewsNation). Brent crude erased most of Day 88's roughly six-percent fall to trade above $99, with the three-tanker LNG line not extending into Day 89 or Day 90 reporting (CNBC, Trading Economics, OilPrice). The 30-day ceasefire probability revises down from 25 to 22; direction shifts to escalating; seven-day risk holds conditional.
