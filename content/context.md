China called the civilian toll a 'red line'; Russia warned of an 'economic earthquake'; Britain designated the IRGC, drawing an Iranian summons of its ambassador. Direction stays escalating; 7-day risk extreme; spillover critical. 30-day ceasefire probability falls to 8.
